Our Mission

Our mission is to develop and support a vibrant ecosystem of robotics capabilities and applications. We plan to do this in two ways:

  1. It is our goal to organize a self-sustaining ecosystem through a community-focused mindset by advocating for open collaboration, providing abundant resources, and lowering the barrier of entry.
  2. We seek to partner with industry and academia to create high-quality robotics technologies and help organizations leverage the power of open tools for their applications.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a dynamic ecosystem of robotics capabilities and resources to empower creators to shape the future through democratising access and open collaboration.
