Services

We also offer services around robotics development and consulting. These activities typically orbit open-source projects, but Open Navigation is open to proprietary feature development to fuel our mission. We offer a discount to member companies and projects that may be made open-source.

ROS Consulting and Development

ROS2 is the standard for robotics development and we're some of the leading experts on it. ROS can be hard and we understand. We offer consulting services make it easier. Whether you need a ROS-based system developed, move your codebase to ROS, or convert your ROS system to ROS2, we have you covered.

Robotics Research & Development

We conduct R&D tasks for organizations in need of seasoned roboticists and engineers. Our team is comprised of creators and maintainers of widely used robot navigation software. We are also uniquely suited to set up or make your enhancements to projects such as Navigation, RViz, Gazebo, MoveIt, and more.

Education and Setup

Open Navigation is a leader in open-source robotics technologies. We offer programs to get your team up and running quickly. We conduct seminars, create on-boarding programs and interactive tutorials, and help set up your team for success.
